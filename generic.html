Solidigm - Hardware Engineer

During my intern tenure at Solidigm, I gained a strong foundation how to visualize data with an oscilloscope. I learned how to automate tests using Keysight's UDA platform and dabbled in PCB layout using Cadence Allegro.

As a full time employee now, my role has me balancing between design and validation. So far, I'm becoming proficient in DfX (ie DFA, DFM, DFT), constraint setup in respect to PCB stackups, and general layout. On the validation side, I'm well versed in low speed embedded communication protcols such as I2C, SMBUS, I3C and SPI.

Westen Digital - Field Applications Engineer Intern

Working out of the San Jose HQ as an intern focused on the flash product portfolio, I had to take initiative to get my own testing setup made. With a simple Raspberry PI and a niche BGA reader, I built an eMMC tester that was based on an employee's Confluence post.

While keeping track of samples and varying programs' product lifecycles (PLCs), I found fulfillment in being able to ship a FFU for an Amazon Kuiper program with my eMMC tester, saving days of delay.
